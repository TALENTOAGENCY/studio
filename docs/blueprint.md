# **App Name**: Talento Careers

## Core Features:

- Job Posting Display: Show job listings with key information such as job title, requirements, and benefits. Display logo and other branding assets according to specifications
- Filtering and Search: Allow users to filter jobs based on keywords, location, experience level, and other relevant criteria. Search across the entire set of job descriptions
- Application Submission: Enable users to submit their applications through the platform, including uploading resumes and cover letters.
- AI Job Match: Use a generative AI tool to match candidates to job openings based on their skills and experience, extracted from resume, and other supplied information.
- Integration with Talento Agency Website: Fetch data and information directly from the Talento Agency website, pulling resources such as images or URLs as needed.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark navy blue (#1A237E) to convey professionalism and trust.
- Background color: Very light blue-gray (#F0F4F8) to provide a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Bright orange (#FF7043) for call-to-action buttons and important highlights.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif for a modern and tech-forward feel.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif for readability and clean presentation of job descriptions.
- Use clear, professional icons to represent job categories and application steps.
- Clean and intuitive layout, emphasizing readability and easy navigation for job seekers.
